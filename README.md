## What is Playr?

Playr is a local music player built for people who own their music and want to hear it without friction. It reads your files, remembers your library, and plays — nothing more, nothing less.

No sign-in. No internet required. No background services. Just your collection, always ready.

---

## Features

- **▶ Play/Pause** — Instant control with a single click
- **📁 Local Library** — Add MP3, WAV, and FLAC files from anywhere on your system
- **💾 Persistent Library** — Your song list survives every restart (stored in a json file)
- **🏷 Metadata Reading** — Automatically reads Title and Artist tags from your files
- **🔊 Volume Control** — Inline slider, no fussing around
- **🗑 Remove Songs** — Clean up your library at any time
- **⚡ Native Performance** — Built with C++ and Qt6 — starts in milliseconds
